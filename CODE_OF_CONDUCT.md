# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, professional, and harassment-free community.

Be respectful, focus criticism on ideas and evidence, assume good intent while correcting mistakes, disclose relevant affiliations, and avoid promotional pressure. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, spam, and repeated bad-faith submissions are not accepted.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect the project. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository security contact when public discussion would expose personal information.
